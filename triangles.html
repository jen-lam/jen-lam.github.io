TriAngles

A mobile application developed to assist university students in the planning and progression of their degrees.

Team: Denise H., Jen L., Kevin L., Kailiang Z., Zhiyu Z.
Role: Research, Development
Duration: June - August 2019

Overview

Background

For a capstone course, we were tasked with designing and developing a software artifact as part of a software development team using agile scrum.


Problem

Many undergraduate students have difficulties understanding their program structure, including potential electives, entry requirements, and relationships between courses. The static information provided in the Handbook and progression plan documents do not provide an engaging eperience for students.
Challenge

How might we assist UNSW students in the planning and tracking of their degrees?
