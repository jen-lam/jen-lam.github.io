WooliesX

Since 2019, I have been working as a user experience and interface designer at Woolworths Group within its digital arm - WooliesX.

If you are not familiar, WooliesX is the digital and innovation hub of the Woolworths Group, one of Australia's largest retailers. Currently working on a registration piece for Woolworths online. Previously supported acquisition and development squads within Woolworths Insurance.
