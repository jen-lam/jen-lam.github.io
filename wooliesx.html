WooliesX

Since 2019, I have been working as a user experience and interface designer at Woolworths Group within its digital arm - WooliesX.

If you are not familiar, WooliesX is the digital and innovation hub of the Woolworths Group, one of Australia's largest retailers. I currently support acquisition and development squads within the Insurance tribe.

Unforunately, a lot of my work thus far is under NDA so I can't show you the actual projects — however, here's what I've been working on in general!
